  1. *EXPERT JUDGMENT  

Expertise should be considered from individuals or groups with specialized

knowledge of similar past projects and the work being performed.  

  1. *DECOMPOSITION 

 Decomposition is a technique used for dividing and subdividing the project scope and project deliverables into smaller,

 more manageable parts. Activities represent the effort needed to complete a work package.  

  1. *ROLLING WAVE PLANNING  

Rolling wave planning is an iterative planning technique in which the work to be accomplished

in the near term is planned in detail, while work further in the future is planned at a higher level.  

  1. *MEETINGS  

Meetings may be face-to-face, virtual, formal, or informal.
